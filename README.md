# Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs customer segmentation using **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis** and **K-Means Clustering**. The objective is to group customers based on their purchasing behavior so businesses can make better marketing and customer retention decisions.

## 🎯 Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to identify which customers are most valuable and which customers are at risk of leaving. This project helps classify customers into meaningful segments for targeted business strategies.

## 📊 Dataset
- Online Retail Dataset
- Customer purchase history
- Invoice details
- Transaction dates
- Quantity and Unit Price

## 👥 Customer Segments

- Loyal High-Value Customers
- Regular Customers
- At-Risk / Lost Customers

## 💼 Business Impact
- Identify high-value customers.
- Improve customer retention.
- Create personalized marketing campaigns.
- Increase customer lifetime value.
- Support data-driven business decisions.
